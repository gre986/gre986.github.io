Amy's sexual frustration in her relationship with Sheldon is a recurring topic in the series. Amy's admiration for Penny has at times bordered on physical attraction. Amy has a PhD in neurobiology, and frequently uses monkeys in her research and experiments. Amy decides to break up with Sheldon in the season 8 finale, becoming increasingly frustrated with his quirks. Bialik herself has a doctorate in neuroscience.
